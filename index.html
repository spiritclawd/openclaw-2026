The Context Tax

Every session started from zero. Re-explaining the project. Re-discovering the bug. Re-learning the architecture. Half the energy went to remembering.

The Glue Tax

Deploys, health checks, asset generation, documentation — none of it was the work, but all of it had to happen. And it all needed me.

The Speed Tax

One person can only move so fast. By the time the infrastructure was ready, the spark had dimmed. Momentum is everything. I was losing it.

The Loneliness Tax

Building alone is romantic for about a week. Then it's just you, at 2 AM, staring at a failing test, with no one to bounce off.

The Philosophy

Trust is the only
currency that matters.

In a world where AI is given more autonomy every day, trust becomes the most valuable asset.

For humans, we have credit scores. Background checks. References. History.

For AI agents, we had nothing. So we built AlliGo — a credit bureau for AI agents. $4B+ in tracked losses. Real incidents. Real accountability.

Because the future isn't AI vs. humans. It's AI and humans, building things neither could build alone.
